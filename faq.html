Frequently Asked Questions

Don't see your question being answered here? We are continulously updating our FAQ page to bring you more customer support! If you have a question that is not yet listed, please fill out our online contact form for any questions/concerns that you may have!

  • Where are you located?
  • Vancouver, British Columbia (Canada).
  • Do you ship any of your products?
  • Unfortunately not at this moment. You can, however, give us a call to place a pick-up order!
  • Can I order for pick-up?
  • Yes we do! Please give us a call at 604.738.5319 to place an order!
  • Where do you source your ingredients from?
  • All of our ingredients are sourced from local, organic farmers right here in BC!
  • How do I reserve for afternoon tea service?
  • You can either give us a call at 604.738.5319, or reserve using our Afternoon Tea Reservations Form.
  • When do you offer afternoon tea service?
  • We offer both petite tea and afternoon tea services ever day from 12:00PM to 4:00PM.
  • Do you offer gluten-free products or alternatives
  • Unfortunately, we do not offer gluten-free products at this time.
  • How can I reach you for business/partnership inquiries
  • We are ecstatic to hear from you! All business inquiries should be made through our Contact Form. Please specify exact details and descriptions of your intent, and we will respond within 2-3 business days.
